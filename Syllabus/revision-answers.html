Lesson 1 : Hardware Components

Define the following

  1. Hardware : The physical components of a computer system which are tangible.
    E.g. Internal Hardware and External Hardware
  2. Software : It refers to the set of programs, which are required to operate the computer system.
    E.g. System software and Application Software
  3. CPU : A central processing unit (CPU) is the circuit that executes instructions of a program. The CPU performs basic arithmetic, logic, controlling, and input/output.

Write a sentence about the following

  1. External Hardware : The Hardware components which are connected to the computer from the outside, these components are also known as peripherals
    E.g. Input, Output and Storage Devices
  2. Internal Hardware : The Hardware Components which are fixed inside the CPU cabinet, and generally harder to remove.
    E.g. Motherboard, CPU,RAM ,ROM, SMPS, Heat Sink
  3. SMPS : Switched-mode power supply,It is also known as the power supply unit. It converts the input high voltage electric current to low voltage and supplies it to the motherboard and other parts of the computer.
  4. Heat Sink : It is designed to disperse the heat generated by the CPU.
  5. Modem : A modulator-demodulator or modem is a computer hardware device that converts data from a digital signal into an analog signal.
  6. Serial Port : A serial port is a serial communication interface through which information transfers in or out sequentially one bit at a time. This is in contrast to a parallel port, which communicates multiple bits simultaneously in parallel.
  7. Parallel Port : A parallel port is a type of interface for connecting peripherals.The parallel ports send multiple bits of data at once.
  8. Microphone is a transducer that converts sound into an electrical signal.
    • A transducer converts a signal from one form of energy to another.

  3. Phishing: is the fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information such as usernames, passwords and credit card details by disguising as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication.

  5. Right to privacy
    No one shall be subjected to random interference with their privacy, family, home, or attacks that affect their honour and reputation. Everyone is protected by law against these attacks/disturbances
  6. Trademark
    A trademark is a type of intellectual property consisting of a recognizable sign, design, or expression that identifies products or services from a particular source and distinguishes them from others.
